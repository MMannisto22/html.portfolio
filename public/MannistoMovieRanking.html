The Best Movies According to Michelle Mannisto

My Top 3 Movies of All Time in No Particular Order

  • Ender's Game

    My husband and I watched this together when we were first starting to be good friends, so I have a lot of good memories associated with it. Plus, the CGI, music score, and acting are amazing!

  • Gladiator

    Oh my goodness, where do I start? This movie makes me feel all the things. The acting, the special effects, the storyline... *chef's kiss*.

  • The Patriot

    This movie always makes me cry but in a good way. The soundtrack is amazing, the storyline is fantastic, and the ending makes you feel good inside.
